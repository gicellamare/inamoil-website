Who We Are

About INAM

Interamerican Oil Company (INAM OIL CO.) is an independent oil and gas company built to find, develop, and produce energy across the Americas. Led by a multidisciplinary team of experienced energy executives with deep regional roots, we combine technical, operational, financial, and commercial capabilities into a single, integrated team — capable of taking a project from evaluation to execution.

Our interamerican DNA gives us a rare perspective: we understand the geology, the institutions, and the business culture of markets that others find complex. That is not a limitation we navigate — it is the advantage we were built around.

How We Operate

Our Approach & Purpose

We believe the Americas hold more energy potential than the market recognizes. Our role is to find it, structure it, and bring it to production — with the creativity, discipline, and integrity that complex markets demand.

As an independent producer, we operate with a level of versatility that larger players rarely can. We are not constrained by rigid mandates or legacy structures — which allows us to look beyond the obvious, identify non-conventional opportunities, and design the financial and commercial frameworks needed to make them viable.

Integrity is non-negotiable. We operate with full financial transparency toward our investors and partners — because trust is the foundation of every project we build.

Beyond energy, we see ourselves as agents of development. The regions where we operate are not just sources of opportunity — they are communities with real needs. We are committed to creating lasting value not only for our shareholders, but for the people and territories that make our work possible.
